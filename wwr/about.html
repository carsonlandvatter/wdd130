ABOUT US

To ignite a spirit of adventure in every soul by guiding unforgettable whitewater journeys that connect people with nature, thrill, and each other. We believe that the river is more than just water—it’s a place where fear turns into courage, strangers become teammates, and every rapid reminds us what it means to be alive.

HISTORY

High River was founded by a group of river guides and lifelong friends who grew up exploring the wild rivers of the Pacific Northwest. What began as weekend adventures quickly turned into a mission to share the thrill of whitewater with others. With just two rafts and a beat-up truck, they launched their first season over a decade ago, fueled by a love for nature, adrenaline, and community. Today, High River has grown into a trusted name in rafting, known for unforgettable experiences, unmatched safety, and a deep respect for the rivers that started it all.
