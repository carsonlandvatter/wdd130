HISTORY

Wild Current Rafting Co. was founded by a group of river guides and lifelong friends who grew up exploring the wild rivers of the Pacific Northwest. What began as weekend adventures quickly turned into a mission to share the thrill of whitewater with others. With just two rafts and a beat-up truck, they launched their first season over a decade ago, fueled by a love for nature, adrenaline, and community. Today, Wild Current has grown into a trusted name in rafting, known for unforgettable experiences, unmatched safety, and a deep respect for the rivers that started it all.
