Overview

Purpose

The purpose behind the site is to allow us to more effectively market our business. We want to attract adventurous people from all around the world to see how fun our business is. This will also allow us to communicate more effectively with our customers and help them get an experience of a lifetime.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Typically, this would target a lot of young adults who physically capable of doing white water rafting and understand how to navigate on a website. A lot of young adults spend so much time on their phones and media that this would attract them the most. What questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They can know how much it costs to go rafting through our site. They can even see pictures of what the rapids might look like, and how to get to them. How will my audience usually access my site? The site will be accessible through a desktop, laptop, and mobile device. This makes it easily accessible to any adult wanting to raft.
